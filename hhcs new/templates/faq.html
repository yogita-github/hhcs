Frequently Asked Questions

Q. What is Health Advisor?
Health Advisor is an online platform serving patients with quality healthcare facility via online consultations.
Q. How can I access this website?
Health Advisor will be available across computers, smart phones and tablets.
Q. Is the patient health data secure?
Yes,patients health data is completely secure.
Q. When can your patients use Health Advisor?
Health Advisor should be used when your patients are looking for non-critical care, a follow up or second opinion, reviewing medical reports, initial treatments etc. An online consultation can sometimes substitute a doctor’s visit.
Q. I am not very technology savvy…what do I do?
It is not meant to be for technology savvy people only. The underlying experience that we have focussed on is the ease of use.
Q. What is the best time to use the website?
The best time to use this website is 24/7! We are always available anytime.
