FAQ

What is Commoners health?

>>Commoners health, is an online platform serving patients with quality healthcare facility via online consultations.

How can I access Commoners health?

>>Commoners health will be available across computers, smart phones and tablets.

When can your patients use Commoners health?

>>Commoners health should be used when your patients are looking for non-critical care, a follow up or second opinion, reviewing medical reports, initial treatments etc.An online consultation can sometimes substitute a doctor’s visit.

Is the patient health data secure?

>>Yes,patients health data is completely secure.

I am not very technology savvy…what do I do?

>>It is not meant to be for technology savvy people only. The underlying experience that we have focussed on is the ease of use.
