Our Story

"Health Advisor" is an online health center to predict a patient's health based on Age, Gender, and body vitals (SBP, HBP, Heart_rate, Glucose, SpO2, Temperature) provided by the users of the portal. All people can access the website free of cost anywhere and everywhere. Since health has always been a prime necessity it is essential to keep a check on it regularly. All the patients can't visit doctors in remote areas where transport facilities aren't available. We, therefore, developed an online website for patients without visiting doctors.
